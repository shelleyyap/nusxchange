About Us

Planning to go on exchange but don't know which school to choose? NUSXchange brings you a wealth of information on the different NUS student exchange programme partner universities. Learn from seniors' experiences via their reviews, or use the search function to filter schools by major, cost or countries, or ask your questions on the comments page!

Alternatively, have you been on exchange and would like to share your experience? Help your juniors out by sharing the highs and lows of your time in SEP and any tips and recommendations that you might have!

This web application is an Orbital project by team P.O.P which conssitst of two members, Chua Ka Yi Ong and Yap Ying Ying.
